About National School of Business and Technology

The National School of Business and Technology (NSBT) is committed to excellence in education and preparing students for dynamic careers in business, engineering, and information technology. Our programs are designed with a blend of academic theory and practical learning to develop leaders of tomorrow.

Vision

To be a globally recognized institution for academic excellence and innovation in business and technology education.

Mission

To empower students with knowledge, values, and skills through modern education and real-world exposure.
